About the project

Sewa-Buddy

"Sewa Buddy" is an android app we developed for our second year "Mobile Applications Development" module.
Customers have the ability to login with their social media accounts and input contact details locally within the app.
They can view ads and make appointments with the respective service provider. After the job is done they can rate the service.
This project was done only for academic purposes.
My part was designing and developing the customer side of the application.
